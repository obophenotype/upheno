OWL Axiomatization
==================

The OWL axioms for HP are in the
[src/ontology/hp](http://phenotype-ontologies.googlecode.com/svn/trunk/src/ontology/hp/)
directory on this site.

The structure is analagous to that of the [MP].

changes in the MP
